Curriculum

Mathbridge Schools uses a modern curriculum that makes learning easy for our students. Our teachers are well trained and our classes equipped. We run a blended curriculum of British and Nigerian.

We adopt the Montessori in our Preschool and it is designed to lay a solid foundation for children aged 18months to 5+. They engage in practical life and sensorial activities like pouring, transferring, sweeping, opening and closing, development of social skills, grace and courtesy.

Our children are prepared to excel in both local and international exams.

Co-curricular activities and sports

Our students engage in different co-curricular like dancing, music and arts. We believe this will help improve the total development of ourstudents.

In addition to this, our students get involved with different sport activities like swimming Aerobics, table tennis and soccer.
