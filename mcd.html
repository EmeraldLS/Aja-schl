About MCD MATHEMATICAL CENTRE

MCD Mathematical Centre is an EdTech company established in 2016 that operates across Africa and Europe. We provide learning and academic alternatives especially in Math and Sciences. Our unique learning program is tailored to deliver the very best both physically and online. Our goal is to empower our clients/learners with the Maths and Science skills they require to excel in their different fields of learning.

MCD Mathematical Centre has conducted over 150,000 hours of tutoring both online and offline and has worked with more than 18,000 students since inception.

Our students have won several local and international awards and scholarships and that is our biggest accomplishment.

How We Work:

Maths and Sciences have always been seen as difficult by so many students. This view can create obstacles to some students’ progress in these subjects. To address this and more, our work procedure includes

  1. Assessment: We assess our students to know their strength and weaknesses.
  2. Counselling: This important procedure help our students get psyched for the journey ahead.
  3. Assigning the Tutor: Based on the assessment and counselling section, the best-fit tutor will be assigned to the student.
  4. Monitoring: We ensure the tutor understands the strengths and weaknesses of the learner. We make learning sessions interactive and engaging. We also track the learner’s performance and report the progress made to the parent/client.

Our Tutorial Package:

Our tutorial service is grouped into 2 categories: Online tutoring and Offline tutoring
